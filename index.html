Previous Event

"See" With the Heart

At Porto UX and Porto Tech Hub’s special accessibility event, participants were blindfolded and guided by Cláudia Barbosa - athlete, nutritionist, and an inspiring force of resilience after losing her sight less than a year ago.

Together, we experienced what it means to navigate the world without vision, reflected on real barriers in digital and physical interfaces, and explored why accessibility, and WCAG, must be at the heart of our design decisions. Design is not just what we see, it’s what we allow others to experience.

Thank you to everyone who joined us on this unforgettable journey of empathy and inclusion.

About Us

Designing with purpose,
growing with community

Since 2016, we’ve been bringing people together to share ideas, stories, and inspiration, from hands-on professionals to curious newcomers.

We believe in people. We believe good design makes a difference; for users and for business. By sharing tools, methods and best practices, we help create better products and real impact.

Behind Porto UX

Marta Fernandez

Marta Fernandez is the co-founder of PortoUX, a designer-led community that has been bringing UX professionals together since 2017. What began as a local meetup has grown into one of Portugal’s most active and engaged UX communities, a space for reflection, sharing, and evolving design practice.

With over 20 years of experience in UX, research, and strategy, Marta’s work explores how design can shape more meaningful, inclusive, and intelligent systems. She’s especially interested in the role of AI in design, and how technology can support clarity, care, and ethical decision-making.

Beyond her community work, Marta writes regularly about design, inclusion, and responsible innovation — including her ongoing Medium series, “Designing AI with Care”.

She created PortoUX with the belief that design grows stronger when we learn together, challenge assumptions, and stay close to what really matters: people.

Behind Porto UX

Alexandra Rocha

Alexandra Rocha is a Digital Product Designer with 20+ years of experience, passionate about creating solutions for complex challenges. She believes user-centered design is the key to improving people’s lives and helping businesses succeed.

Alongside her design work, Alexandra has been mentoring UX professionals for years, supporting their growth while learning just as much from the experience. Since 2018, she has also been a board member of PortoUX, where she helps plan talks, workshops, and mentoring programs with the aim of fostering growth and bringing people together.

This year, she began sharing her thoughts on Medium, starting with her reflections on “10 Principles to Keep UCD Alive in Fast-Paced Teams”, with the goal of making user-centered design more practical, impactful, and human.
